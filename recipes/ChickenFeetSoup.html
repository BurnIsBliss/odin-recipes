Chicken Feet Soup

The following steps are to be followed to create the one of the most sought after delicacies in the world:

  • Step 1

    • Get ur chicken feet from your trusted vendor/butcher (the more dirty the feet the more tastier the soup will be)

  • Step 2

    • Hoping that you have secured all the necessary ingredients for a basic soup. We will dive down to create the broth:

  • Step 2.1

    • In a pan get some Chicken blood (for color) and water and heat it until boiling hot temperature.

  • Step 2.2

    • Add your spices and ur condiments to taste to this solution.

  • Step 2.3

    • Keep an eye on the mixture so that it does not evaporate entirely, cause we are trying to make our broth.

  • Step 3

    • Add your UNCLEANED Chicken legs into your broth. And let it sit there for a significant amount of time. Let the dirt and microbes get into the broth to bring the flavours together.

  • Step 4

    • Serve it hot in a soup bowl.

    Chef's Note: Add more blood according to your taste and appetite. Recommended to have it hot.
